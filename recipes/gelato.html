Gelato

Description

Made with quality ingredients such as milk, cream and eggs, this dark Chocolate Gelato Recipe is the best chocolate ice cream you'll ever taste. It's rich, thick creamy and authentic. Perfect for every chocolate lover. You'll love it!

Ingredients

  • heavy cream
  • milk
  • 5 egg yolks
  • salt
  • dark chocolate
  • cocoa powder
  • sugar
  • vanilla bean

Steps

  1. Heat the milk and cream gently. Cut the vanilla pod in half and run the knife along the inside of both halves scraping the vanilla beans out
  2. Add the vanilla beans, pod, milk and cream into a heavy-based saucepan and set over low heat on the stovetop
