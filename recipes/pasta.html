Pasta

Description

This recipe will delve deep into what makes the perfect dish of penne pasta. Anyone can prepare penne out of a box, but it takes true artistry to create pasta from scratch. This won't be easy, but with our help you will soon be able to admire and taste all of your hard work.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • flour

Steps

  1. Place flour in a mound on a flat clean surface.
  2. Crack 4 eggs into a well in a center of the mound.
  3. Incorporate the flour into eggs until dough forms
  4. Bring the dough together with palms and knead for 5 minutes
  5. Once the dough starts to form a ball, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for atleast 30 minutes
  6. Cut the dough into four equal pieces, cover each with a towel
  7. Flour the work surface and roll each ball of dough into a large thin sheet
  8. Once rolling out the dough, fold the dough on the lightly floured surface over itself several times. Cut the roll into strips depending on the type of pasta you are making.
  9. Dust flour on sliced strips and unravel
  10. Repeat with the remaining dough
